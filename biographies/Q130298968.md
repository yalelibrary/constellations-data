# Theodore E. Hill
## Graduate of Yale College, 1904
Theodore E. Hill was born in 1879 in Williamston, North Carolina to Edward Hill and Rose Keyes Hill. He attended Phillips Andover before coming to Yale, and graduated with a bachelor's degree from Yale in 1904. He continued his education at the New York Law School, graduating in 1907. He began a career as an attorney in New York City, where he was also a member of the cityâ€™s Republican Club. He died a few years later, in 1910, from heart failure.
