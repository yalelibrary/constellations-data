# Alfred Louis Jackson
## Attended Yale College 1904-1907

Alfred Louis Jackson was born on July 3, 1884, in Washington, D.C. His father was John Louis Jackson, a waiter at C. & O. Railroad, and his mother was Lula Dabney. He attended the M Street High School in Washington, D.C. During his time in high school, he assisted in editing an issue of _The Colored American_.[^1] He graduated from the M Street High School in 1904 and enrolled at Yale College.[^2] At Yale, he was a member of the Washington Club at Yale.[^3] He was at Yale from 1904 to 1907 and left in March of 1907 due to poor health. He returned home to Washington D.C. where he died on June 12, 1910.[^4]
